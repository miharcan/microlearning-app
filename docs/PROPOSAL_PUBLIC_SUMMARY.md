# Public Proposal Summary

## Context
The product direction is based on a context-aware student digital twin concept for personalized microlearning.

## Public Summary
- Microlearning opportunities are identified in short real-world windows.
- Interventions should be timely, safe, and learner-controlled.
- Learning should combine retrieval practice, spacing, and mastery progression.
- Insights should serve both learner progress and educational decision support.
- Privacy and consent must be first-class design constraints.

## Why Only Summary Is Shared Here
The full proposal includes strategic and implementation-sensitive material.
To protect IP and commercialization readiness, only a public-level summary is included in this repository.
